Our Story

Founded in 2010, Chillica brings together innovative culinary techniques with time-honored traditions. Our chef, Marco Bellini, combines global influences with locally-sourced ingredients to create a dining experience like no other.

Our philosophy is simple: exceptional ingredients, masterful techniques, and heartfelt hospitality. We believe that dining is more than just sustenance - it's an experience that engages all the senses.

Our space was designed with comfort and elegance in mind. The warm, contemporary atmosphere creates the perfect backdrop for memorable meals and special occasions.
